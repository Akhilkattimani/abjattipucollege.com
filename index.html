Welcome to Our College

Providing quality education for a better future.

Shree A.B.Jatti PU College is a prestigious Pre-University institution affiliated with the Bharatiya Lingayat Development Educational (BLDE) Association, Vijayapura. Established in 1969, the college has been a beacon of academic excellence for decades. Named after the distinguished statesman and former Vice President of India, Shri A.B.Jatti, the college carries forward his legacy of dedication to education and public service. The college offers Science, Arts, and Commerce streams with highly qualified faculty committed to nurturing students into well-rounded individuals ready to face the challenges of tomorrow.

Facilities for Sciences stream

  • Laboratories: Well-equipped Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Computer labs for practical learning.
  • Integrated Coaching: Many PU colleges provide coaching for NEET, JEE, KCET alongside regular classes.
  • Library Access: Updated textbooks, reference materials, and digital resources.
  • Counseling & Career Guidance: Dedicated cells to help students plan higher education paths in engineering, medicine, or pure sciences.
  • Hostel & Canteen: Safe accommodation and healthy food options for outstation students.
  • Sports & NCC/NSS: Encouragement for physical fitness and discipline through extracurriculars.

Facilities for Arts stream

  • Humanities Resources: Libraries stocked with history, political science, sociology, psychology, and literature texts.
  • Debate & Cultural Clubs: Platforms for students to develop communication, leadership, and creativity.
  • Guest Lectures & Seminars: Exposure to social sciences, law, journalism, and civil services preparation.
  • Counseling & Career Guidance: Support for careers in law, UPSC/KPSC, journalism, teaching, and social work.
  • Extracurricular Activities: Theatre, music, and arts festivals to encourage holistic development.
