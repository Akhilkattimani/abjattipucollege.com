Eligibility Criteria

  • Must have passed SSLC (Class 10) from Karnataka or an equivalent exam recognized by the Government of Karnataka.
  • Minimum marks requirement varies by stream:
  • Science: Higher cut-off, especially for PCMB/PCMC combinations.
  • Arts: Relatively flexible, but merit still matters.
  • Fill in personal details, stream preference (Science/Commerce/Arts), and attach required documents.

Documents Required

  • SSLC marks card (original + photocopies).
  • Transfer certificate (TC)
  • Caste/Income certificate (if applicable for reservation).
  • Aadhaar card.
  • Passport-size photographs.
